Harvest Nepal

Harvest Nepal is a dedicated platform that aims to empower farmers and promote sustainable agriculture practices in Nepal. With a mission to enhance the agricultural sector, we provide valuable resources, information, and tools to support farmers in making informed decisions and maximizing their yields.

What we do?

  1. Suggest which crop to plant according to your soil chemicals.

  2. Tell what fertilizer to use for the perfect yield of crops.

  3. Predict which disease your crop may have.

Our Services

Crop Recommendation System

This system suggest us to grow suitable crops on basis of our soil chemicals.

Fertilizer Recommendation System

This system helps us to use perfect fertilizer on basis of our soil chemicals and yield more production.

Crop Disease Finder

This system helps us to detect the diseases on plant leaf and suggest solutions to cure it.
